Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my recipes across devices?

Recetio automatically syncs your recipes using iCloud. Make sure you're signed in to the same iCloud account on all your devices.

How do I create a weekly menu?

Go to the "Weekly Menu" tab and drag recipes from your collection to the desired meal slots. You can also add custom text for meals.

Can I share my recipes?

Yes! You can share individual recipes as PDF or share shopping lists with family and friends.

How do I organize my recipes?

Use categories and tags to organize your recipes. You can filter by favorites, categories, or search by ingredients.
